About Malaysia Tourism

Welcome to the official tourism page of Malaysia. We are dedicated to showcasing the beauty and diversity of Malaysia’s natural landscapes, cultural heritage, and vibrant cities.

Join us in exploring the essence of Malaysia, a country that offers an array of experiences across cultures, landscapes, and adventures.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: To inspire travelers by showcasing Malaysia’s unique cultural, natural, and urban wonders.

Vision: To establish Malaysia as a premier sustainable travel destination, celebrated for its diversity and hospitality.

Our Story

From ancient rainforests to modern cityscapes, Malaysia has developed into a top global travel destination. Our tourism board has grown to embrace sustainable travel, bringing Malaysia’s rich history, diverse culture, and modern attractions to travelers from all over the world.

Key Milestones:

  • Launch of eco-tourism initiatives in 2000
  • Recognition as a top travel destination in 2015
  • Global sustainability award in 2022
